You need to decide: build vs buy, microservices vs monolith, this framework vs that one.

Here's how to decide well.

## The Framework

**1. Define the problem**
What exactly are you solving? If you can't state it clearly, you can't solve it.

**2. Gather context**
What do you know? What don't you know? What's the timeline?

**3. Identify options**
At least three. Never have only two choices. The third often emerges as the best.

**4. Evaluate with criteria**
What matters? Speed? Cost? Scalability? Maintainability? Pick the criteria that actually matter.

```
└─────────────┴──────────┴──────────┴─────┘
```

**5. Decide and commit**
At some point, decide. Don't infinite loop. Commit and move.

**6. Review later**
Was this the right call? What did you learn?

## The Secret

Perfect decisions don't exist. Good decisions are made well and reviewed honestly.

Don't overthink. Don't underthink. Decide.
